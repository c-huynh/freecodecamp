Anthony Bourdain

The main who ate his way through the world, and brought us along for the ride.

His life:

  • 1956 - Born in New York City, New York, US
  • 1978 - Graduated from the Culinary Institute of America
  • 1998 - Became executive chef at Brasserie Le Halles
  • 2000 - Released bestselling book Kitchen Confidetial
  • 2002 - Premier of A Cooks' Tour, based on his book of the same name
  • 2005 - Premier of No Reservations
  • 2011 - Premier of The Layover
  • 2018 - Death by suicide
